Who Are We

We are young. We are brave. We are the Youth

Rotaract Club of Dombivli Suncity is a Rotaract club parented by Rotary Club of Dombivli Suncity. The club was chartered by the stunning star Rtr. Vaibhav Vispute approximately three years back. Rotaract Club of Dombivli Suncity was formed with a motive to provide better opportunities for the youth of Dombivli, who rather had limited options for their crucial development.

The club possesses an exceptional quality of brains and professionals. Over the past 3 years, the club is known for providing the moment with some of the most stellar Rotaractors. In the year 2016-17, the club is proving with 6 DCMs, which includes 2 Core team Members at the district level, enough said! We are the exceptional rockstars in the world of Rotaract too.

We are a noble organisation!!! We have dedicated a team of experienced rotaractors to take care of all our social causes and initiatives, known as team community service.

The right balance of working professionals and students provides the right amount of maturity and energy to the club. Well, having said that we also party a lot. We have a team of party experts, who raise the level of expectations every time. We call them team Club service.

Rotaract Club of Dombivli Suncity is famous for its seriousness towards child education initiative. The club has always kept education and professionalism development as its thrust area. Rotaract Club of Dombivli Suncity is also known to be the most happening club in the Rotaract district 3142. The quality of the club service and community service projects has attracted many eyeballs.

In RC Dombivli Suncity every person finds some or the other interesting opportunity for their self-development to join the club. So don't delay! Fill the membership form today.

What We Do

Help the community & make new friends. Develop your skills & connect globally.

Like every Rotaract club, we do a lot of social work but, always in a fun way. Our social work is generally concentrated towards the cause of education, women empowerment, social empowerment, regional empowerment, environment, modernization, nation buildup and everything you can think of, cause for us even Sky is not the limit. In the process, we also associate ourselves with a lot of NGOs, Government and private institutions.

Rotaract is a multinational youth organisation and our initiatives are practised always towards the humanity and environment. So if you are a young enthusiast and love to contribute to the society then join us.

We also take into account that it's a competitive world and youth needs to exercise their skill sets and acquire new too. We here at Rotaract help you acquire these skills knowingly or unknowingly. There is a pool of departments, which will help you in acquiring skills in your favourite stream. For gaining experience in the field of digital media, you can join Web communication avenue, for accounts, there is team finance and for marketing, you can simply lay your hands in PR & Marketing avenue so on and so forth.

After so much of intense learning arises the need for an equally intense recreational platform. This is taken care by our Club Service department. Every month, not less than once, the club reunites together to either trek, party, outings, seminars, competitions and every other thing you can think of.

So join us today and challenge yourself to the extremes.
